Top Questions and Answers About Diamonds

Welcome to the I Trust Faiz FAQ, featuring clear and honest answers to help you make the best diamond decision possible.

What are the 4Cs of diamonds?

The 4Cs—Cut, Color, Clarity, and Carat—are the core measures used to evaluate a diamond. At I Trust Faiz, we help you understand each in plain terms so you can make a confident choice that fits your budget and your occasion.

What are lab-grown diamonds?

Lab-grown diamonds are real diamonds created in high-tech labs. They share the same structure as natural diamonds but are made in weeks, not millions of years. At I Trust Faiz, we focus exclusively on natural diamonds for their rarity and value.

What does conflict-free mean?

All I Trust Faiz diamonds are sourced ethically, with zero ties to conflict regions. We ensure every diamond is certified conflict-free through international standards like the Kimberley Process.

How many diamond shapes are there?

There are many shapes, including Round, Oval, and more. Each has its own personality—choose the one that speaks to you.

What is a loose diamond?

A loose diamond is not yet set in jewelry. At I Trust Faiz, you can select your perfect diamond and then choose or custom-design the setting it belongs in.

Where should I buy a diamond?

From a trusted expert. At I Trust Faiz, you get GIA-certified stones, transparent advice, and one-on-one service—every time.

Which diamond shapes are most popular?

Round is always classic. But today, ovals and cushions are also leading the way. We help you pick the best one for your look and lifestyle.

What is a certified diamond?

Certified diamonds are graded by independent labs like GIA. Every diamond we offer at I Trust Faiz comes with documentation to prove its quality and characteristics.

How do I buy a loose diamond?

It’s simple. Contact us, tell us what matters to you, and we’ll curate the perfect diamond for your needs—plus walk you through every step personally.

Are diamonds durable?

Diamonds are the hardest natural material on Earth—ideal for everyday wear, especially in engagement rings.

What jewelry looks best with diamonds?

Anything! From classic solitaires to tennis bracelets and custom necklaces, diamonds elevate every piece they touch.

How are diamonds set in jewelry?

Most rings use prong or or bezel settings that hold the diamond securely while showcasing its brilliance. We help match settings to stone with care.

How can I tell if a diamond is real?

Real diamonds disperse heat quickly, resist scratching, and sparkle uniquely. Or skip the guesswork—buy from I Trust Faiz, and rest assured every stone is authentic and certified.

Is it safe to buy diamonds online?

Yes—if you buy from a trusted source. At I Trust Faiz, every diamond is handpicked and comes with full certification, expert support, and complete transparency.
